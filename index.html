Biography


Zuhui Wang is currently a 2nd-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Stony Brook University (SBU), advised by Dr. Zhaozheng Yin. Zuhui received his Master degree in Computer Science at University of Central Florida in 2016, and also earned his Bachelor degree in Computer Science and Technology at Southern Medical University in 2009.

He is currently interested in biomedical image processing, multimodal analysis, and video representation.
